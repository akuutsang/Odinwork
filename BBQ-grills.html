salmon topped with cilantro, jalapeno, and butter.

By TRACIANDJIGGER

Ingredient;

  • 3 pounds salmon
  • Â¼ cup butter
  • 1 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1 fresh jalapeno pepper, seeded and chopped
  • Old Bay Seasoning TM to taste

Directions

Step 1; Preheat grill for high heat.

Step 2; Lightly grease one side of a large sheet of aluminum foil. Place salmon on the greased side of foil. Melt the butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Remove from heat, and mix in cilantro and jalapeno. When cilantro is wilted, drizzle butter mixture over the salmon.

Step 3; Place foil with salmon on the grill. Season with Old Bay. Cook 15 minutes, or until fish is easily flaked with a fork.
